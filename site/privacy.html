Privacy Policy

Last updated June 21, 2026

The short version

This is a static, independent comparison site. There are no accounts, no advertising, no tracking pixels, and no analytics. We don't want your data and we don't collect it.

What we store

One thing, and only in your browser: your light/dark theme choice, saved to localStorage as aa-theme. It never leaves your device, and we have no way to read it.

Hosting & logs

The site is delivered through Cloudflare, which may process standard request metadata (such as IP address and user-agent) for security and content delivery, governed by Cloudflare's own privacy policy. We do not run our own server-side analytics or store visitor logs ourselves.

Images & assets

Platform logos/favicons are self-hosted (downloaded and served from this site), so simply loading the page does not call third-party trackers to fetch them.

External links

Links to the listed platforms and to GitHub lead to third-party websites that have their own privacy policies. We're not responsible for their content or practices.

Cookies

We set no cookies. (Cloudflare may set a security cookie at the edge; see its policy above.)

Children

This site is not directed at children and knowingly collects no personal information from anyone.

Changes & contact

If this policy changes, the "last updated" date above will change with it. Questions or corrections? Open an issue on GitHub.
